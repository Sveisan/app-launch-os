Privacy Policy

Breathe Collection is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our premium iOS breathwork, meditation, and wellness app.

1. Information We Collect

Breathe Collection does not collect invasive personal data. We do not require you to create an account with us. Your identity and access are managed through your Apple ID and the App Store. We may receive anonymized or aggregated data necessary to operate the app, such as subscription status and app usage analytics, which we use solely to improve the service.

2. Apple ID and Authentication

We do not maintain a native user account system. Your authentication is handled entirely by Apple. We do not store your Apple ID, email address, or any personal identifiers. Access to premium features is verified through Apple's in-app purchase system.

3. Subscriptions and RevenueCat

In-app purchases (Monthly, Yearly, and Lifetime subscriptions) are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, a third-party subscription infrastructure provider. RevenueCat helps us verify your subscription status and restore purchases across devices. Financial transactions are securely handled by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store or have access to your payment information; all payment data is processed by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

App preferences, progress, and settings may be stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, some of this data may sync across your personal Apple devices via Apple's infrastructure. This data remains under your control and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: (a) Apple, for app distribution, authentication, and payment processing; (b) RevenueCat, for subscription management and purchase verification. Each of these providers has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy and RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for more information.

6. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., when contacting support). We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Breathe Collection is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any data we process. Financial and transactional data is handled by Apple and RevenueCat using industry-standard security practices.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. You can manage your subscription and associated data through your Apple ID account settings. To delete app data from your device, uninstall the app. For requests related to data we may hold, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy within the app or on our website. Your continued use of Breathe Collection after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the support options provided in the app or on our website.

Effective Date: March 2025
