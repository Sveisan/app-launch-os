## 🎨 Brand Guide: "Quiet Luxury"

The Breathe Collection aesthetic is built on the concept of **Quiet Luxury**—premium, understated, and functional. It avoids "gamification" bloat in favor of discipline and focus.

### Core Philosophy
- **Scientific Rigor**: Every protocol must be verifiable (e.g., PubMed, Cell Reports).
- **Minimalism**: Remove everything that isn't the breath. No ads, no sleep stories, no bloat.
- **Sensory Focus**: Designed for **eyes-closed sessions** using haptic feedback and high-fidelity soundscapes.

### Visual Identity
- **Background**: `#000000` (True Black)
- **Primary Color**: `#2C7873` (Deep Teal - Stability & Trust)
- **Secondary Color**: `#52AB98` (Ethereal Teal - Calm & Air)
- **Accent Color**: `#E07B39` (Warm Orange - Energy & Focus)
- **Typography**: **Outfit** (Google Fonts). Values weights 300 (Light) for elegance and 600 (Semi-Bold) for hierarchy.

### Tone of Voice
- **Professional & Expert**: Sounds like a hybrid between a neuroscientist (Andrew Huberman) and an elite coach.
- **Action-Oriented**: Focuses on mechanics and protocols rather than abstract "spirituality".
- **Direct**: Short, punchy sentences that respect the user's time and intelligence.

## 🎙️ Voice & Visual Guidelines

To maintain the "Quiet Luxury" standard across media, follow these specific parameters for voice generation and image creation.

### The Voice: Conversational Momentum
- **Brand Standard**: Use the **"Original / Liam"** profile (ElevenLabs ID: `WKuirq2f3xMEda412HQt`).
- **The "Meditation Trap"**: Avoid hyper-calm or "sleepy" voices. Breathe Collection requires conversational momentum and authority to hold attention in high-pacing environments like TikTok/Reels.
- **Rhetoric**: Use **ellipses (...)** for contemplative weight and **short, grounded sentences** to provide gravitas.
- **Settings**: Stability: **0.5-0.7** | Clarity/Boost: **0.75-0.85**.

### The Visuals: Atmospheric Immersion
- **Aesthetic**: **Ultra-Glass** and **Breathing Auras**.
- **The Orb**: The central breathing orb is the "heartbeat" of the app. It should feature smooth, organic gradients and deep radial blurs (`filter: blur(35px)`).
- **Environment**: Dark, minimalist backgrounds that allow the light and textures of the "glass" components to stand out. Avoid cluttered or realistic stock photography.
